Scope of Service & Results

No CTR Guarantees: We do not guarantee a specific Click-Through Rate boost or a certain number of views.

Best Effort: We promise to put our best creative effort into every design to give your video the highest chance of performing well.

02
Payment & Refund Policy

Non-Refundable: Due to the digital nature of the work and the time required for custom creation, all payments are non-refundable.

Final Sale: Once work has started or the final file is delivered, the transaction is considered complete.

03
Delivery & Timelines

Standard Turnaround: We aim to deliver within 24 to 48 hours.

Potential Delays: If delivery takes longer than committed, we will inform you immediately via email.

Rush Orders: Requests for delivery faster than the agreed timeframe may incur an additional Rush Fee.

Major Overhauls: Requests for a completely different concept after first draft are treated as a new order.

Client Assets: You must ensure you have the right to use any images or logos you provide.

Client Responsibilities

Clear Briefs: Please provide high-quality photos and a clear description of your vision.

Response Time: Delays in providing feedback will result in delays in final delivery.

07

Ownership Transfer: Full usage rights are only transferred upon receipt of full payment.

Fraudulent Chargebacks: Any "service not received" chargeback after delivery will be contested.

09
Right of Refusal

We reserve the right to refuse service for reasons including unclear communication, payment history issues, or requests that violate YouTube's Community Guidelines.

10

Final Approval: Once the final high-resolution file is sent, the job is considered "Approved". Changes after this point require a new order.

Abandoned Projects: If a client goes silent for more than 20 days without notice, the project is marked "Abandoned" and any payments are forfeited.
